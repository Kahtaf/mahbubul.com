About the Author

Mahbubul Alam is born and raised in Bogra, Bangladesh. He comes from a family of five sisters and one brother. He completed his M.Sc in Mechanical Engineering at the Byelorussian Polytechnic Institute, Minsk, Byelorussia (formerly USSR). He currently lives in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. On his leisure time he researches and writes about various aspects of science and religion.
